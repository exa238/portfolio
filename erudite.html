Erudite

About the Project

The purpose of Erudite is to create a space where students can share their academic experience in a way that they want to prepare for their courses. Erudite's goal is to function as a digital studying system and a social space for students to share their materials pertaining to their courses. With Erudite, students can seek this help easily and accessible and do not have to endure the hassle of struggling to find sourcing around the web. It’s all in one place.

Problem & Solution

The Problem

The purpose of Erudite is to create a space where students can share their academic experience in a way that they want to prepare for their courses.

The Solution

The goal of Erudite is to function as a digital studying system and a social space for students to share their materials pertaining to their courses. With Erudite, students can seek this help easily and accessible and do not have to endure the hassle of struggling to find sourcing around the web. It’s all in one place.

Target Audience

Students

Students who are looking to collaborate and expand their knowledge on certain subjects.

Teachers

Educators who want to construct a digital studying space for their students to prepare for exams and quizzes.

Tutors

Tutors who want to organize sessions with students and share materials to help them better understand the subject at hand.

Design Options & Decisions

Task Lists

  • The user will login or sign up for an account within Erudite
  • The user will choose 2 subjects of interest.
  • The user will navigate the home page to find which community they would like to join
  • The user will determine which study group they would like to join
  • The user will view their list of communities

User Goals

  • Easily navigate the homepage of the app without confusion
  • Be able to see your completed list of study groups
  • Easily sign up for a study group

User Testing

  • 2 participants: 1 Female (Age 35), 1 Female (Age 53)
  • Testing the Erudite app, designed to help students book a study group to discuss courses and share materials.
  • Participants were ask to complete the following task: Login and book a study group
  • Average time to complete the task was 47 seconds:
    • Participant 1 (35): 42 seconds
    • Participant 2 (53): 53 seconds
  • 100% of participants were able to complete the task

Revision

  • Changed the label of the “Book a Community Group” section
  • Reorganized which section come first on the home screen

Constraints & Limitations

  • Throughout my process, I was felt constrained by Figma or what I knew how to do within Figma. One example includes making an interactable keyboard and chat system feature that my user could interact with. Another example includes connecting Erudite with your own person calendar apps.
  • However, I am confident that I would be able to accomplish these things in the future after growing my understanding.

Next Steps

  • In the future, I plan to conduct more user testing on different demographics (gender identities, ages, technology proficiency, etc.)
