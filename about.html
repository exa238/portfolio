About Me

Hi! I'm Emily, a UX Engineer and Interactive Media Designer who enjoys creating experiences that blend thoughtful design with technology. I love building websites, interactive installations, and digital products that are both intuitive and engaging.

My background in Computer Science, Screenwriting, and Interactive Media allows me to approach projects from both technical and creative perspectives. Whether I'm designing interfaces, prototyping ideas, or writing code, I'm always thinking about the people using what I create.

When I'm not designing you'll find me tending to my diverse collection of houseplants, reading, experimenting with new vegan recipes, playing cozy games, or exploring ways to combine storytelling with technology.
